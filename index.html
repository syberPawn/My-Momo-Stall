How it works

Order on WhatsApp. Pay securely. Pick up by token.

  1. Tap the WhatsApp button and send Hi.
  2. Select a menu item and quantity.
  3. Confirm the order summary.
  4. Pay through the secure Razorpay link sent by Domoz.
  5. Receive your Domoz token on WhatsApp after successful payment.

Cancellation and refund policy

Customers may cancel before payment. Once payment succeeds and preparation starts, cancellation may not be available. If Domoz cannot fulfil a paid order due to item unavailability or operational issues, customers can contact Domoz on WhatsApp for refund support. Eligible refunds are processed back through the original payment method where possible.

Privacy policy

Domoz stores the customer's name, WhatsApp number, order details, and payment status to manage orders and pickup tokens. Payment information is processed by Razorpay; Domoz does not store card, UPI PIN, or bank credentials.

Terms

Menu availability and prices may change. A Domoz order is confirmed only after successful payment. Customers must show their WhatsApp token at pickup. Orders are prepared for the selected token date shown in the WhatsApp confirmation.
